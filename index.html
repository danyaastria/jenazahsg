What to do

When Your Loved One
Passes Away

Losing someone is not an easy thing to overcome. As A Muslim, we must always remain patient (Sabr) and believe in Allah S.W.T.

If Death Occurs at Home:

1. Call Saith Services and I will advise you on the next steps.
2. Have a Doctor to certify the death. Once Doctor is there and goes through the procedure, you will be given a letter to certify the cause of death.
3. Make a death certificate here.
4. In the mean time, I would have reached your place of residence or advice you on the next procedure.

If Death Occurs in the Hospital:

1. Consult doctor or nurses if the Jenazah can be released from hospital and collected by family.
2. If no post mortem is needed, call Saith Services and we will be at the hospital as soon as possible.
3. From the mortuary, we will meet up with family members and discuss about time of burial, bathing of jenazah etc.
